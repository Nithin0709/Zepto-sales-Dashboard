# Zepto-sales-Dashboard
This project analyzes 2,000+ synthetic e-commerce sales transactions to uncover trends in customer behavior, product performance, sales distribution, and revenue growth. The goal is to convert raw transactional logs into actionable business insights using Python, Power BI, Data Visualization, and EDA Techniques.

## Project Objectives
Understand revenue contribution across categories, products, and cities

Identify top-selling and low-performing items

Study purchasing trends based on time, payment mode, and quantity patterns

Analyze customer purchase frequency & revenue contribution (RFM-like approach)

Build a Power BI dashboard for business decision support

## Key Insights from EDA
Revenue Growth - 	Month-wise revenue trend shows demand fluctuations and sales peaks
Top Categories - 	Electronics & Fashion lead in total revenue contribution
Customer Behaviour - 	Few customers generate majority of purchases (Pareto effect)
City-wise Sales - 	Metro cities contribute the highest revenue numbers
Payment Trend - 	UPI/Card usage dominates over COD

## Dashboard Features (Power BI)
Monthly Revenue Growth
Top Performing Categories & Products
City-wise Revenue Distribution
Payment Type Share Distribution
Customer Revenue Summary
